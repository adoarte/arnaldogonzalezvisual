Arnaldo Drés González (b. 1986, Caracas - Venezuela).

Since 2014 lives and works in Hamburg, DE 


Education

2016 Master in Fine Arts, Hochschule für Künste im Sozialen Ottersberg, DE. Teacher's class: Prof. and Visual Artist Michael Dörner.

2011 Bachelor in Fine Arts, Mixed Media Degree (Cum Laude Honors) Universidad Nacional Experimental de las Artes, UNEARTE (Formerly: IUESAPAR), Caracas, VEN . Teacher's class: Visual Artists Nan González, Jorge Dominguez-Dubuc and Marcos Salazar Delfino.
