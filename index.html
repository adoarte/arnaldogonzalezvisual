Item Title

Arnaldo Drés González is an artist who explores the fragility of belonging and transit through a practice born from Venezuelan instability and displacement in Europe. His work, at the intersection of the digital and the traditional, delves into aesthetics of gloom and intervened materiality—from worn archives to his own body—to construct poetic and abstract discourses. Inviting introspection, he reveals the layers of fragmented identity and the persistence of memory in the search for a transcendent home.
